Safer Dating badge

The Safer Dating Badge shows you’ve taken a moment to pause, learn, and commit to safer, more respectful connections – for yourself and for others.

To earn this badge, you’ll:

  1. Watch a short information video (under 5 minutes)
  2. Answer a few quick questions covering the essentials of safe, consent-based dating

Key topics include:

  • Setting and respecting boundaries
  • Understanding clear, enthusiastic consent
  • Recognising red flags and unsafe behavior
  • Preparing for in-person meetups safely
  • Using check-ins and support tools

Once completed, the Safer Dating Badge will appear on your profile, letting others know you’re someone who takes mindful connection seriously.
