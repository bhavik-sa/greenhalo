Social Connect badge

The Social Connect Badge helps build trust and authenticity in your dating experience because real connections start with real people.

To earn it, simply connect 3 of your social accounts (like Instagram, LinkedIn, TikTok, Facebook, etc.). Once linked:

  • We’ll verify your accounts are legitimate
  • You control your visibility: choose to share full URLs with specific connections, or let others see a blurred preview with follower/friend count only
  • You earn the Social Connect Badge, signalling transparency and digital presence

It’s a subtle but powerful way to show you’re genuine, active, and here for meaningful connection – not games or ghosting.

Your identity, your control. Their peace of mind.
