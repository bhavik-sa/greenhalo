Halo'd Profile badge

The Halo'd Profile Badge is a sign that you're showing up with authenticity, intention, and integrity. It tells others that you've taken the time to create a well-rounded, trustworthy profile - and you're here for a mindful, respectful connection.

To earn your Halo'd Badge, simply complete the following:

  • Connect 1 social account (e.g. LinkedIn, Instagram, etc.)
  • Add 1 dating app you've used — or let us know if you're a non-app dater
  • Complete your full personality profile
  • Submit 2 references (from friends, family, or colleagues — it's your call)

Once these steps are completed, your Halo'd Profile Badge will appear automatically - giving others confidence in who you are and how you show up.

You're not just dating. You're dating with clarity, care, and credibility.
