Green Flagged badge

The Green Flagged Badge is a powerful signal that you show up with respect, kindness, and integrity, and others have noticed.

To earn it, your last 2 date connections simply need to give you a thumbs up review after meeting or chatting. It's quick, anonymous, and rolling - so your badge reflects how you've been showing up recently, not just in the past.

This badge says:

  • You communicate with respect
  • You honour boundaries and consent
  • You leave people feeling safe and seen

It's not about perfection, rather it's about presence, intention, and emotional safety. Dating should feel good, and you're part of making that happen.
